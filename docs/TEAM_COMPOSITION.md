## üí° Recommendations

### **Choose Normal Team If:**
- ‚úÖ Well-funded (Seed/Series A)
- ‚úÖ Need fast time-to-market
- ‚úÖ Competing in crowded space
- ‚úÖ Enterprise customers expected
- ‚úÖ Compliance requirements (SOC2, HIPAA)

### **Choose Minimal Team If:**
- ‚úÖ Pre-seed or bootstrapped with some runway
- ‚úÖ B2B SaaS with longer sales cycles
- ‚úÖ Technical founders who can contribute
- ‚úÖ Focused on product-market fit
- ‚úÖ Can accept slower velocity

### **Choose Ultra-Lean Team If:**
- ‚úÖ Fully bootstrapped
- ‚úÖ Technical founders doing most work
- ‚úÖ MVP validation phase
- ‚úÖ Side project ‚Üí full-time transition
- ‚úÖ Need to prove concept before raising
