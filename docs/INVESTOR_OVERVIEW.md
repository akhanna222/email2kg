# Email2KG - Investor Overview

## üéØ Executive Summary

Email2KG transforms unstructured email and document data into actionable business intelligence using AI-powered OCR and knowledge graphs. The platform automatically extracts, categorizes, and connects information from thousands of documents, enabling instant insights that typically require hours of manual work.

### The Problem

- **$15B+ annually** spent by businesses on manual document processing
- **60-70%** of business-critical information trapped in unstructured emails and PDFs
- **2-3 hours/day** wasted by knowledge workers searching for information
- Traditional OCR solutions have **15-30% error rates** on complex documents
- No automated way to discover relationships between documents and entities

### Our Solution

Email2KG is an intelligent document processing platform that:
- Achieves **98-99% OCR accuracy** using GPT-4 Vision
- Automatically processes **email attachments** from Gmail, Outlook, and IMAP
- Builds **knowledge graphs** showing relationships between entities
- Provides **instant search** across all documents and extracted data
- Scales to handle **millions of documents** with async processing
