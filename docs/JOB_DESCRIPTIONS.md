## Engineering Manager / Tech Lead

### About the Role

We're looking for an experienced Engineering Manager to lead our technical team and drive the development of Email2KG, an AI-powered platform that transforms emails and documents into intelligent knowledge graphs. You'll be responsible for technical architecture, team leadership, and ensuring we deliver high-quality software that scales.

### Responsibilities

**Technical Leadership:**
- Define and evolve system architecture for scalability and maintainability
- Make critical technical decisions on frameworks, tools, and approaches
- Conduct code reviews and ensure engineering best practices
- Guide technical roadmap and prioritization

**Team Management:**
- Lead and mentor a team of 5-8 engineers
- Conduct 1-on-1s, performance reviews, and career development conversations
- Facilitate sprint planning, retrospectives, and agile ceremonies
- Foster a culture of collaboration, innovation, and continuous improvement

**Delivery & Quality:**
- Ensure on-time, high-quality delivery of features
- Monitor and improve engineering metrics (velocity, quality, uptime)
- Balance technical debt with feature development
- Coordinate with Product and stakeholders

### Requirements

**Must Have:**
- 8+ years of software engineering experience
- 3+ years of engineering management experience
- Deep expertise in Python (FastAPI/Django) and modern backend technologies
- Experience with PostgreSQL, Redis, and distributed systems
- Strong system architecture and design skills
- Proven track record of building and scaling teams
- Experience with agile/scrum methodologies

**Nice to Have:**
- Experience with AI/ML systems and OpenAI APIs
- Background in document processing or knowledge graphs
- Experience scaling SaaS products from 0 to 100K+ users
- Contributions to open-source projects

### Tech Stack

- **Backend:** Python, FastAPI, PostgreSQL, Redis, Celery
- **Frontend:** React, TypeScript
- **AI/ML:** OpenAI Vision API, LangChain
- **Infrastructure:** Docker, AWS (EC2, RDS, S3), Nginx

### What We Offer

- **Salary:** $150K - $200K based on experience
- Equity: 0.5% - 1.5%
- Health, dental, vision insurance
- Unlimited PTO
- Remote-first culture
- Professional development budget
- Latest hardware (MacBook Pro, external monitor, etc.)

### Interview Process

1. **Recruiter Screen** (30 min) - Culture fit, expectations
2. **Hiring Manager Interview** (60 min) - Technical background, leadership experience
3. **Technical Deep Dive** (90 min) - Architecture discussion, system design
4. **Team Interviews** (2 x 45 min) - Meet the team, cross-functional collaboration
5. **Final Interview** (45 min) - CEO/Founder, vision alignment

### Requirements

**Must Have:**
